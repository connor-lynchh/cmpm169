Experiment 2 - Vector Art, Animation & Interactivity

Imitate

I started with looking at the examples on the generative design website.

I choose the example that looked most intresting to me and started modifying it

Integrate

With this code I now have I wanted to have my own shapes in places of the ones that were given in the example.

This required me to draw triangles, rectangles, ellipses as well as custom shapes. I also had to use the random function to make the color of the shapes change.

Innovate

To innovate on this example and create something new I wanted to go further with my random color generation.

Beyond there being a key to change the color of the shapes you can now move your cursor to one of the four quadrants on the grid to change the color.

On top of that when the color changes when moving your cursor to a new quadrant the previous shape stays behind until its covered up by other shapes.
