Experiment 5 - 3D Graphics

Imitate

I started with looking at the examples that were given in lecture but none of them seem to have grab my attention. I then got an example off open processing that more alligned with my vision. I used the code from the example as a foundation to start my experiment

Integrate

After going through lecture and learning all the different ways to edit and change the way 3D graphics look in p5.js. I impelemented lighting and 3D objects and had the objects moving on the x, y, and z axis.

Innovate

After changing a lot of my design and going from a couple of 3D objects moving up and down I came to this pong type idea. I really enjoyed the direction the experiment went as before the pong art I was not particularly satisfied with the program. So I am glad I was able to pivot into something familiar but on a whole new perspective.
