Experiment 4 - Images, Video, & Sound Art

Imitate

I started looking at design processing examples and searched for video examples. I used one of the numerous examples as a foundation for the experiment I wanted to work on.

Integrate

After essentially stripping out most of the code other than the webcam functionality I started integrating sound effects and images that layered over the webcam video.

Innovate

After going through the hardships of trying to make tint() work I found another solution to getting the color of the image to change when colliding with the border of the canvas.

With that now set in place I finally had a functioning DVD screen saver but instead you have yourself as the DVD logo. On top of that a sound effect will play when hitting one of the four corners of the canvas.
